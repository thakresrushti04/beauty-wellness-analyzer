# 💄 Beauty & Wellness Industry Analyzer

## Overview
Analyzed 8,000+ Sephora beauty products to identify brand performance, 
ingredient trends, and customer rating patterns across categories.

## Problem Statement
- Which beauty brands dominate each category?
- What price range gets the highest ratings?
- Which categories have most customer engagement?

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle — Sephora Products and Skincare Reviews
- Products: 8,216 across 9 categories
- Price range: $3 to $1,900

## Tools & Libraries
Python | Pandas | NumPy | Plotly | Google Colab

## Visualizations Built
- Sunburst Chart — Brand hierarchy by category
- 3D Bubble Chart — Price vs Rating vs Loves Count  
- Treemap — Product distribution by sub-category
- Violin Plot — Rating distribution by category
- Heatmap — Average rating by category and price range

## Key Insights
- Makeup and Skincare dominate product count
- Men's category has highest average ratings (4.62 at $0-25)
- Expensive Makeup ($200+) underperforms with only 3.0 avg rating
- Niacinamide Serum at $6 has 763k loves — budget products win
- Tools & Brushes most consistent ratings across all price ranges
